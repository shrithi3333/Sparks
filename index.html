About Us

FARMING IS NOT JUST A JOB IT'S A WAY OF LIFE!

Save Indian Farmers ! Our organization was established to address issues related to farmer's suicides in India. The organization is funded by people like you who work hard and are also socially conscious about issues. Our organization collects the donation money to help the farmers with their basic needs. The donated money is used to provide the farmers with automatic equipments for farming easily, providing food to the farmers and their families thrice a day and giving them some amount of money for their personal requirements. 40% of Indian farmers have an improved situtaion than before thorugh this initative.A small donation from your side can change someone's poor life to a better life.
